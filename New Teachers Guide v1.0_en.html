Before Applying for ARC

You must complete a medical health check FIRST. This costs around 100,000 KRW and includes tests for TBPE and various drugs including marijuana.

Note: HIV tests should not be mandated.

Pro Tips
  • No reservation needed for ARC applications (as of 2023)
  • For Seoul, Suwon, Incheon: Many walk-in visitors arrive before 9am and queue. The earlier you arrive, the shorter your wait time
  • Coordinate with your school for timing
  • Bring cash only - cards not accepted

Registration Deadline
  • Education Office registration required within 10-15 days of starting work (varies by region)
  • Your ARC is needed, so apply for ARC promptly after arrival
  • If delayed, your employer can explain the situation to the Education Office
