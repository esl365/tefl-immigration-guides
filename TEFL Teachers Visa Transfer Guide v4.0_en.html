Critical Deadline

Must visit Immigration Office within 15 days of starting new job!

  • Make an appointment as soon as possible (high demand)
  • If unable to book online: walk-in on the 15th day (excluding first working day)
  • Gathering and submitting documents is YOUR responsibility
For Education Office Registration:

Your new employer will need:
Apostilled Degree Certificate
Apostilled Criminal Background Check

Don't have copies? Submit other documents at immigration first, then request copies.
